--- title: Distinguish Benign and Malign Tumor via ANN author: andrea perlato date: '2019-02-05' slug: distinguish-benign-and-malign-tumor-via-ann categories: - artificial intelligence tags: - ANN ---

We try to recognize cancer in human breast using a multi-hidden layer artificial neural network. We use the Wisconsin Breast-Cancer Dataset which is a collectioin of Dr.Wolberg real clinical cases. There are no images, but we can recognize malignal tumor based on 10 biomedical attributes. We have a total number of 699 patients divided in two classes: malignal and benign cancer.
